04

Preferred logo type

There are seven principal types of logo. Mark any that appeal to you — or leave it to us to recommend the strongest fit.

Wordmark
The full company name as distinctive type — Google, Coca-Cola.
Lettermark
Initials or a monogram — IBM, HBO, NASA.
Pictorial mark
A recognizable icon or object — the Apple, the Twitter bird.
Abstract mark
A geometric form with no literal meaning — Pepsi, Adidas.
Mascot
An illustrated character that personifies the brand.
Combination mark
Symbol and text paired together — Burger King, Lacoste.
Emblem
Text enclosed within a badge, crest, or seal.
